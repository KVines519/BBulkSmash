About BBulkSmash

BBulkSmash provides a modern web platform for SIPp and Homer, making professional and portable VoIP testing accessible to everyone — from beginners to experienced engineers. BBulkSmash is not just a GUI for SIPp; it integrates the bulk call generating capabilities of SIPp with the analysis and packet drill-down capabilities of Homer.
